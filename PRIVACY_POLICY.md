# Privacy Policy for WebAudit Extension

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## Overview
WebAudit is a browser extension designed to perform security audits on web pages. This extension operates entirely client-side and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Data Collection and Usage
- **No Data Collection:** WebAudit does not collect any personal information, browsing history, or user data.
- **Client-Side Operation:** All analysis is performed locally within your browser. No data is sent to external servers.
- **Permissions:** The extension requires permissions to access the active tab and inject scripts for auditing purposes, but this data remains local to your device.

## Data Storage
- WebAudit does not store any data persistently. Audit results are displayed in the extension's interface and can be exported as JSON files at the user's discretion.
- Exported reports are saved locally on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services
WebAudit does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.
